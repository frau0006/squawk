Welcome!

A particular 30-year-old Floridian Linux Boomer once said that anyone with something to say should have their own personal website, so I made one. Right now the metal image I have of this site can be illustrated by someone flinging a black garbage bag into the woods.

My name's Cole! Some people call me Spekk, too. :) I'm a programming student who has changed majors twice in two years. Acquianted with C++ since ca. 2013, I'm now expanding my knowledge base in technology onto Java, HTML/CSS, Bash scripting and the wonderful world of VIM.

Now as I sit at home, waiting for the woohoo pandemic to blow over, I adventure into my social-media detoxification process, endeavours of self-mastery and trying way too hard not to get absolutely memed by le chad stoicism.

The people they ask me: "Cole where do you want to be in 10 years?" and I say: "very big garden." And then the people they ask me: "Cole who do you think you are?" and I go "NOOOOOOOOO STOP MAKING ME CONSUUUUMMMMEEEEEEE" and then they say "shut yuor up idiot!!" and i say "ok"

They shall say, "Why would you make this website?" and I shall wisely respond: "In a non-canonical Gospel, Jesus was once approached by a man who asked him: Rabbi, why are you throwing car batteries into the sea? And Jesus replied: the sea is right here, and I don't want them." The logic is very similar.
